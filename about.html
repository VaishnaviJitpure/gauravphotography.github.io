About me- GAURAV MANKER

A tale weaved in vibrant colours and pictures remains with the human mind, it being visual by design.

Somewhere towards the end of 2019, Gaurav decided he had to make this vision a reality. Thus was born Gaurav Photography, a candid Pre-wedding photography and portfolio venture. Built on the tenets of uncompromising quality and commitment that Gaurav honed in hisself as an Student of Physical Education. Gaurav is an avid traveler, loves reading and enjoys long drives. He also loves teaching and conducts workshops.
